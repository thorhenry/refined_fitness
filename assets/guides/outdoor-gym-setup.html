Outdoor Gym Setup Guide

1. Site Selection

  • Level ground required (max 2% slope)
  • Minimum area: 15' x 15' (4.5m x 4.5m)
  • Good drainage essential
  • Consider sun exposure and shade
  1. Clear vegetation and debris
  2. Level the ground
  3. Add crushed stone base (4" depth)
  4. Install rubber matting or artificial turf
  • 6ft between equipment pieces
  • 8ft clearance for moving parts
  • Safety zone marking around each station

2. Surface Preparation

  1. Clear vegetation and debris
  2. Level the ground
  3. Add crushed stone base (4" depth)
  4. Install rubber matting or artificial turf

3. Equipment Layout

Recommended spacing:

  • 6ft between equipment pieces
  • 8ft clearance for moving parts
  • Safety zone marking around each station

4. Installation Requirements

Installation Checklist:

  • Concrete foundation (minimum 6" depth)
  • Proper anchoring hardware
  • Weather-resistant coating
  • Safety caps on all bolts

5. Maintenance Schedule

Frequency Tasks
Weekly Visual inspection, tighten loose parts
Monthly Deep clean, lubricate moving parts
Quarterly Surface inspection, rust prevention
