Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our fitness equipment

Treadmills & Cardio Equipment

How often should I lubricate my treadmill belt?

+

For optimal performance, lubricate your treadmill belt every 3 months or after 130 miles of use. Follow these steps:

  • Clean the belt surface thoroughly
  • Apply silicone-based lubricant only
  • Distribute evenly across the belt
  • Run the treadmill for 5 minutes to spread lubricant
Note: Over-lubrication can damage the belt. If unsure, consult your manual or contact support.

Why is my treadmill display not working?

+

Common causes include: loose power connection, tripped circuit breaker, or console malfunction. First, check all connections and reset the machine. If issues persist, contact our technical support.

How do I calibrate my exercise bike's resistance?

+

Enter calibration mode by holding down the resistance up/down buttons for 5 seconds. Follow the on-screen prompts to set minimum and maximum resistance levels. For magnetic bikes, this may require technical assistance.

Strength Training Equipment

How often should I inspect cable machines?

+

Inspect cables weekly for signs of wear, fraying, or damage. Clean pulleys monthly and lubricate moving parts every 3 months. Replace cables immediately if any damage is detected.

What's the weight capacity of your equipment?

+

Weight capacities vary by model: Standard benches support 300-600lbs, power racks 1000-1500lbs, and cable machines 200-400lbs per station. Check your specific model's manual for exact specifications.

Installation & Setup

What's the best surface for outdoor equipment?

+

We recommend rubber matting or shock-absorbing surfaces with proper drainage. Concrete or level paving stones work well. The surface should be level and stable. Avoid direct soil contact to prevent rust and instability.

How do I protect equipment from weather?

+

Use weather-resistant covers when equipment is not in use. Apply rust-prevention coating bi-annually. Ensure proper drainage around equipment and check for rust monthly. Store removable parts indoors during severe weather.

Maintenance & Care

How often should I check outdoor equipment?

+

Perform weekly visual inspections for rust or damage. Tighten all bolts monthly. Clean equipment bi-weekly and apply protective coating every 6 months. Check safety features before each use.

What cleaning products are safe to use?

+

Use mild soap and water for regular cleaning. Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage protective coatings. For metal surfaces, use specialized outdoor equipment cleaners. Always dry thoroughly after cleaning.

Safety & Usage

What are the safety guidelines for outdoor gyms?

+

Always inspect equipment before use, maintain proper form, use safety features provided, and avoid use during severe weather. Keep children supervised and follow posted weight limits and usage instructions.

Can I customize my outdoor gym layout?

+

Yes, our equipment is modular and can be arranged in various configurations. Consider space requirements, safety zones, and proper anchoring. Contact our design team for custom layout assistance.
