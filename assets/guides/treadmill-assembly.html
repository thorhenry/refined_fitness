Treadmill Assembly Guide

Professional Series - Model RF-2024

⚠️ Safety First

  • Assembly requires 2 people
  • Clear a space of at least 8' x 4'
  • Keep children and pets away during assembly
  • Wear appropriate footwear

Required Tools

  • Allen wrench set (included)
  • Phillips screwdriver
  • Adjustable wrench
  • Level

Assembly Steps

Step 1: Unpack and Verify Components

  • Main treadmill deck
  • Console assembly
  • Upright posts (2)
  • Hardware kit
  • Safety key
  • Power cord
  • User manual

Step 2: Install Upright Posts

  1. Carefully lift the treadmill deck
  2. Connect the console cables through right post
  3. Attach posts using 4 bolts each side
  4. Do not fully tighten bolts yet

Step 3: Mount Console

  1. Connect all cables to console
  2. Carefully tuck cables into console mast
  3. Secure console with 4 bolts
  4. Now fully tighten all frame bolts

Step 4: Final Setup

  1. Level the treadmill using adjustable feet
  2. Install safety key
  3. Connect power cord
  4. Test all functions at low speed

Final Checklist

  • All bolts are fully tightened
  • Console is firmly attached
  • Treadmill is level on floor
  • Safety key functions properly
  • Emergency stop button works
  • Display powers on correctly

Important Notes

  • Maximum user weight: 350 lbs
  • Required ceiling height: 8 feet minimum
  • Keep area behind treadmill clear (6 feet)
  • Perform monthly maintenance checks
